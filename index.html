Hey, welcome.

My name is Prakash Kumar. I’m currently building Clipwise.ai and VideoCompany.ai.

I’m a tech entrepreneur with a deep love for coding and building products from first principles. Previously, I co-founded Rheo TV and Relevel.

At Rheo TV, we built cutting-edge distributed live-streaming systems. We raised funding from top investors including Sequoia’s Surge and Lightspeed Ventures, and the company was eventually acquired by Unacademy.

Post-acquisition, I co-founded Relevel with Unacademy, where I built the product and tech organization from the ground up. Over time, we grew the team to 40+ people and scaled the business to $10M in ARR before my departure.

I write these blogs as my public notes—insights, learnings, and reflections, including the mistakes I’ve made along the way.
